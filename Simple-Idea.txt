Simple-Idea.txt
overview: 
Mod that performs a similar function to pmmo or runescape profession leveling, adds a bunch of passive stat buffs for tasks based off experience gained by performing the specific task (i.e chopping trees gives experience in the lumbering profession, and a level in the lumbering profession might give +2% wood chop speed).

Profession Ideas:
Agility: running
Quarrying: mining stone
Mining: mining ore
Excavation: digging dirt, sand, etc
Fishing: ....fishing
Farming: planting crops
Building: placing blocks
Magic: enchanting
Swimming: swimming
Combat: dealing dmg to hostile mods
